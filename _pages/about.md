The goal of the Dosimetry Core Unit (DCU) is to support effective and safe radionuclide therapy for cancer patients. To this aim, we perform translational research, covering the whole range from bench to bedside to community. Knowledge and expertise is used within the DCU to work on three important sub-goals:

1. Accurate absorbed dose calculations for safe and effective radionuclide therapy for personalized treatment in patients.
2. Development of new suitable tracers for radionuclide therapy, photodynamic therapy and pre-clinical dosimetry.
3. Obtaining fundamental knowledge about dose-effect relationships.
