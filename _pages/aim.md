<section class="section-intro">
	<p>
		The Dosimetry Core Unit (DCU) supports safe and effective radionuclide therapy through
		translational research across the full pathway from bench to bedside to community.
	</p>
</section>

<div class="content-grid">
	<article class="content-card">
		<h3>Personalized Dose Calculation</h3>
		<p>
			Advance accurate absorbed dose calculations to improve treatment personalization,
			effectiveness, and safety for patients receiving radionuclide therapy.
		</p>
	</article>

	<article class="content-card">
		<h3>Tracer and Method Development</h3>
		<p>
			Develop and validate novel tracers and dosimetry methods for radionuclide therapy,
			photodynamic therapy, and preclinical applications.
		</p>
	</article>

	<article class="content-card">
		<h3>Dose-Effect Biology</h3>
		<p>
			Generate fundamental knowledge on dose-effect relationships to strengthen biological
			interpretation and support better clinical decision-making.
		</p>
	</article>
</div>
